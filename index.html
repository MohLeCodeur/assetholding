Our Story

From real estate roots to integrated asset management.

Founded in 2018, Asset Holding initially focused on real estate development and project promotion before evolving into a broader multi-asset investment platform.

Over the years, Asset Holding has worked on development projects, structured transactions and long-term holdings, constantly refining its approach around discipline, risk management and aligned interests with its investors.

Today, we bring together real assets, financial structures and strategic advice, always with the same objective: to build and preserve value over time, away from noise and short-termism.

Key milestones (example):
  • 2018 – Creation of Asset Holding, real estate focused
  • Expansion into structured investment vehicles
  • Development of private mandates for European investors
  • Integration of advisory, real assets and loan structures
Our Mission

Guide, structure and transmit long-term capital.

Each client situation is unique. We combine real estate, financial instruments and customized structures to meet risk appetite, timeline and objectives. Our team works closely with legal and tax experts to ensure that each transaction makes sense, both economically and structurally.

Collective Management Mandates: Pooling Opportunities.

These investment vehicles allow capital to be pooled to target specific assets, often inaccessible individually.

For investors seeking diversified exposure with active management. These closed structures allow Asset Holding to deploy complex investment strategies in targeted markets.

We ensure group cohesion and deployment strategy, based on strict selection criteria and total alignment of the interests of all participants. Monitoring is regular and transparent.

Application Framework

  • Closed-end investment structures.
  • Open only to qualified investors.
  • Predefined acquisition and exit strategy.
  • Transaction cost optimization through scale effect.

Customized Private Management: Personalized Strategy.

We address the complex needs of families, family offices and ultra-high net worth investors with unique solutions.

Unlike standard products, our private mandates are unique roadmaps, integrating the tax environment (particularly in relation to Switzerland), estate planning and liquidity requirements specific to each estate.

The objective is to optimize growth and ensure wealth transmission in a secure and professional framework. The approach is holistic, covering financial and real assets.

Our Commitment

  • Holistic approach (wealth, taxation, succession).
  • Integration of legal experts in Switzerland.
  • Performance reports adapted to Family Office constraints.
  • Absolute confidentiality and discretion.

Equity Investment: Active Participations.

Asset Holding identifies equity investment opportunities in high-potential projects or unlisted companies.

Our approach is that of a strategic partner, involved in governance and value creation of the company or project. We are not passive investors, but growth facilitators.

Each equity stake is structured with a clear vision of the exit horizon and capital protection mechanisms (oversight rights, structured governance). We invest where we can positively impact performance.

Key Criteria

  • High growth potential.
  • Structured and aligned governance.
  • Investor capital protection mechanisms.
  • Defined medium/long-term exit strategy.

Debt Structures and Structured Financing.

We design financing solutions adapted to the specific needs of our projects or those of our partners.

These financial structures can range from secured senior loans to more flexible mezzanine financing. Debt is an essential tool for optimizing equity returns, and our structures are meticulously calibrated.

Financing terms (covenants, guarantees, collateral) are specifically negotiated by our team in Switzerland to minimize default risk and secure lent capital against project cash flows.

Financing Types

  • Senior and secured loans.
  • Flexible Mezzanine financing.
  • 'Bridge' loans for short-term liquidity needs.
  • Guarantees and securities on real estate or real assets.

Real Estate Assets: Value and Development.

Our historical expertise is based on real estate, covering acquisition, development of complex projects, and long-term asset management.

We favor markets where fundamentals (demographics, economy) justify sustainable valuation. We focus on creating tangible value through development or strategic repositioning of assets.

We are not speculators, but builders of real estate value. Each asset must present a robust business plan and resilience to economic cycles.

Areas of Intervention

  • Development of residential and commercial projects.
  • Repositioning of existing assets (Value-add).
  • Holding of assets generating stable income.
  • In-depth market analysis before any commitment.

Strategic Advisory and Project Positioning.

This service goes beyond simple advice. We help project sponsors refine their investment proposition and attract capital.

Asset Holding supports developers and sponsors upstream of their operations. This includes assistance with structuring legal and financial vehicles, economic modeling and creation of professional documentation.

Our network allows us to ensure targeted and effective fundraising, by positioning the offer with investors whose profile matches the project's needs (the targeted "Marketing" of the operation).

Key Services

  • Audit of the project's legal and tax structure.
  • Development of investment documentation (IM, pitches).
  • Assistance in negotiating Term Sheets.
  • Targeted networking for capital raising.

Proposal investing

How we work with potential investors.

We treat every new relationship as a long-term partnership, not a one-shot transaction.

Before proposing an investment, we take the time to understand the investor's profile: the origin of their wealth, their risk appetite, time horizon, expectations and constraints.

We then structure a proposal that may include one or more strategies (loan, equity, co-investment, real estate exposure, etc.), always with a clear risk/return logic and documented scenarios.

Once alignment is found, we formalize the conditions in a clear framework (Term Sheet, agreement, guarantees) and only after that, we move to execution.
